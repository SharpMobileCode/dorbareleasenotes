Alternative Trail List Views
You can now change the way the trail list looks! Tap on "More", and under "Settings" you can select how the trail list looks.
Bottom Navigation Menu Scrolling
When you now scroll through the trails list, the bottom navigation will slide away, allowing more room for the trails list. Scroll back up to bring back the navigation menu.
Weather Alerts
Weather alerts (if any) will now show up in the Weather section of the trail details page. Tap on the alert title for more details.
